Act 4 — The honest part

Speed is not the same as wisdom.

You can now build faster than you can think. That's a new kind of risk. You can ship something that technically works but solves the wrong problem. You can fall in love with your own velocity and mistake movement for progress.

The AI doesn't know what matters.

  • It doesn't know your users.
  • It doesn't have taste.
  • It can't tell a good experience from a technically correct one.
  • It doesn't feel the difference.

That's still you.

The tools changed.
The instinct is still yours.
