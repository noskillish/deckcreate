## 4. Transcribe™ — Free Transcription Tool

I don't have anything fancy to say about this.

I just built Transcribe™. A free transcription tool, because I don't like the ones out there.

Upload an audio or video file. Get a transcript with timestamps.

That's it. It's free while tokens last.

The pricing model is community-driven:
- There's a shared token pool.
- Anyone can use it.
- Hopefully some people donate and pay it forward to keep it running.

When the pool runs out, transcriptions pause until it's topped up again.

No accounts. No subscriptions. No upsells. No ads. No pop-ups.

Simple. Free. Community-funded.

## 12. Wiretap™ Guardian — Phone Scam Protection

$3 million.

That's how much a group of phone scammers managed to get by simply calling people.

Phone scams are still big business. Between $500 billion to $1 trillion globally. And you can understand why:

Scammers get direct access to victims by voice, often with no one next to them.

Victims are ripe for social-engineering tactics.

And there's absolutely no protective layer between the victim and the scammer.

So, I decided to build one: Wiretap™ Guardian.

Building on my smart AI voicemail application, Wiretap™, I can now have my agent listening in on all calls not on my verified callers list.

This means I get instant warnings by SMS if a scam attempt is suspected.

It's a friction point between the recipient and a potential scammer. A small line of defense in a moment when most people have none.

## 13. Wiretap™ — AI Voicemail

Voicemail sucks.

So I built my own. Sound on. No robotic prompts that drag on. No menus that make you tap through the whole phone tree. No system that feels frozen in 1998.

And no important messages buried behind all of it.

Wiretap takes the message, understands context and delivers quick. Everything is logged in my Dashboard, if I want to go deeper. Full access to everything.

Since I'm using AI to process the message, I can also scan for different things like:
- Scam or sales calls
- Appointments
- Shopping requests

And much more.

**Stack used to built it:**
- Frontend: Vanilla HTML/CSS/JS
- Backend: Node.js, Express (Local), Netlify Functions (Prod)
- Database: Supabase (Prod) / SQLite (Local)
- AI: ElevenLabs, OpenAI (Voice generation, STT, TTS, Analysis, text generation)
- Phone: Twilio (SMS, Calls)

Decided to build it, because [Telia](https://www.linkedin.com/company/teliacompany/), [Norlys](https://www.linkedin.com/company/norlys-dk/), [OiSTER](https://www.linkedin.com/company/oister/), [Telmore](https://www.linkedin.com/company/telmore/), [Telenor](https://www.linkedin.com/company/telenor-group/) and many others didn't bother to do it.
